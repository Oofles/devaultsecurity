Florida Air National Guard

Jul 2008 - Present

I am also a current member of the Florida Air National Guard. I work as a cyber security analyst and incident responder for the [601st Air Operations Center's](https://www.1af.acc.af.mil/Library/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/289621/601st-air-and-space-operations-center/) Mission Defense Team. Mar 2019 - Aug 2019
